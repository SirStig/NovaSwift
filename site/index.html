The 2002 classic, rebuilt natively for Apple Silicon.

EV Nova is one of the deepest space trading-and-combat games ever made — and it's stranded on PowerPC. NOVA Swift rebuilds the whole game from scratch in Swift on Metal: resource parser, flight and combat sim, AI, missions, economy and UI. Not a wrapper, not an emulator — it reads your own data and plays in your pocket.

A real, playable game

Not a tech demo — a game you can lose.

On your own data, you can already fly the campaign end to end. The galaxy-day clock ticks as you fly, so the world lives around you.

Fly & fight

The original Newtonian flight model, AI ships spawned from the real fleet tables, ionization, target-lock, and combat-odds decision making.

Explore & trade

Plot courses across the real galaxy map and burn real fuel on jumps, then land to trade, outfit and buy ships against a persistent pilot — mass-based pricing, slot limits, paid repairs.

Play the campaign

Take and complete missions. Background events and news fire, mission ships spawn and fight, and storylines actually progress as days pass.

Lose for real

Run your armor to zero and you eject in an escape pod — or it's game over. Wreck the wrong government's ships and your record follows you.

Live in the world

Meet named përs captains with their own quotes and grudges, hire escorts on a daily wage, and gamble on the holovid races.

Honest about the gap

The gap is fidelity, not features. AI and spawning were never open-sourced, so ours is reconstructed — flight smoothness is the top of the backlog.

Things the original never had

Because it's our code now.

Fidelity comes first — a pure Classic run behaves exactly like the original. Everything modern is an opt-in layer on top, never a replacement.

A live Story Map

A pannable, zoomable graph of every campaign in your data, resolved against your pilot's actual progress.

Multiple pilots, side by side

Roll up pilots from any starting scenario and switch between them — every save is backed up automatically.

The one rule

You bring the game.

We ship code; you supply the data. EV Nova's content is still owned by ATMOS, so this project contains zero copyrighted game data and never will. NOVA Swift reads your own legally-owned copy at runtime — classic resource forks, .ndat, or the modern BRGR .rez container — the same model as OpenMW and OpenRA.

Getting started

Build it in a few commands.

Requires macOS with the Xcode command-line tools. Your EV Nova data stays git-ignored and never leaves your machine.

Clone & fetch dependencies

Run scripts/setup.sh to pull the open-source dependencies.

Drop in your data

Place your legally-owned EV Nova files into data/base/.

Build & run

Open app/NovaSwift.xcodeproj in Xcode, then build for Mac, iPad or iPhone.

# fetch open-source dependencies scripts/setup.sh # place your EV Nova data into data/base/ # (see docs/GET_THE_DATA.md) # optional: free community plug-ins scripts/fetch-plugins.sh # build & test swift build && swift test
